# FAQ

## Why can a great company have a low Opportunity Score?
Because the position may be overweight, technically weak, expensive, or inferior to another use of capital.

## Why does the strategy keep cash?
To preserve optionality during pullbacks and avoid forced selling.

## Why does a sell ladder start above market?
Normal harvest orders should execute only after the stock moves in your favor.

## Why is ROI different from time-weighted return?
ROI measures your wealth against contributions. Time-weighted return isolates strategy performance from cash-flow timing.
