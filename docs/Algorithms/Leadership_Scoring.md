# Leadership Scoring

Current leadership measures market behavior, not intrinsic business quality.

The historical scanner used:
- price above 20-, 50-, and 200-day averages
- 50-day average above 200-day average
- 1-, 3-, and 6-month absolute returns
- 1-, 3-, and 6-month relative returns vs. QQQ

Typical action bands:
- 90–100: Attack
- 75–89: Accumulate
- 60–74: Hold
- below 60: Replace candidate

Strategic-core safeguards may override automatic demotion.
