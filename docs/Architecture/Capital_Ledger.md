# Capital Ledger

The capital ledger records external cash flows separately from investment activity.

## Event types
- initial contribution
- additional contribution
- withdrawal
- external transfer
- fee not already reflected in account value

## Excluded events
Internal buys, sells, dividends retained in the account, and position rotations are not external capital events.

## Purpose
Support accurate personal ROI, time-weighted return, money-weighted return, and cash-flow-matched benchmarking.
